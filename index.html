How to Store Homemade Chocolate Chip Cookies

We can almost guarantee you won’t have leftovers—this is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe around! If you do end up with any extra chocolate chip cookies, they can easily be stored for later enjoyment. To keep your leftover cookies chewy and soft, store them at room temperature in resealable food-storage plastic bags or stacked in tightly covered containers.

If you want to freeze your homemade chocolate chip cookies instead, wait until they’ve cooled completely. Stack them in between layers of wax paper in a covered freezer container or plastic freezer bag and freeze your cookies for up to 2 months. To thaw, keep them covered at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours. Go ahead and chow down!
